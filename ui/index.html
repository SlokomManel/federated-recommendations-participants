Privacy Notice

Please read before continuing

How This App Works:
This application uses Federated Learning to provide personalized Netflix recommendations while keeping your data private.

Your Data Stays Local:
Your Netflix viewing history and preferences are processed locally on your device. Only anonymized model updates are shared, never your raw data.

What We Collect:
- Your recommendation choices (which shows you click on)
- Optional feedback ratings
- Local preferences stored in your browser

Your Control:
You can clear your click history at any time from the History page. All browser data can be deleted by clearing your browser's local storage.
